How to Measure Your Foot

Follow these simple steps to ensure you select the perfect size for your PACE footwear.

1

Prepare

Place a piece of paper on the floor against a wall. Stand on it with your heel lightly touching the wall.

2

Mark

Have someone mark the longest part of your foot (heel to longest toe) on the paper with a pen.

3

Measure

Measure the distance from the edge of the paper to the mark in centimeters (CM) to find your size.
